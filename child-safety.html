Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: July 7, 2026

Premier Uni Central is committed to providing a safe environment for all members of the Premier University community. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

Our Standards

  • Users may not create, upload, share, request, or distribute content involving child sexual abuse or exploitation.
  • Users may not use the platform to groom, exploit, or endanger minors.
  • Any account found violating these standards may be permanently suspended or removed.
  • Content reported by users is reviewed by authorized administrators.
  • Where required by applicable law, reports involving child sexual abuse material or exploitation may be reported to the appropriate authorities.

Reporting

Users can report inappropriate posts or comments directly within the application using the built-in reporting feature.

Reports are reviewed by authorized administrators as quickly as reasonably possible.

Enforcement

Premier Uni Central reserves the right to remove content, suspend accounts, or permanently ban users who violate these Child Safety Standards.
