Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

Premier University Central ("the App") is a community platform designed exclusively for students of Premier University. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

By using Premier University Central, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Username
  • Profile photo (if uploaded)
  • User-generated posts
  • Comments and replies
  • Private messages
  • Blocked users list
  • Reports submitted for posts or comments
  • Subscription status
  • Device information necessary for app functionality

2. Authentication

User authentication is securely handled through Firebase Authentication. Your login credentials are managed by Firebase and are not stored directly by the developer.

3. Database

Your account information and app content are securely stored using Google Firebase Cloud Firestore.

Stored information may include:

  • User profile information
  • Posts
  • Comments
  • Messages
  • Reports
  • Announcements
  • Notification records
  • Other data required for normal app functionality

4. RevenueCat & Google Play Billing

The App uses RevenueCat together with Google Play Billing to manage premium subscriptions.

RevenueCat may process subscription-related information such as:

  • Purchase status
  • Subscription expiration
  • Entitlements
  • Anonymous purchase identifiers

No credit card or payment information is stored by the App or the developer.

5. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Authenticate users.
  • Display your profile.
  • Allow users to create posts and comments.
  • Provide private messaging.
  • Display university routines.
  • Show official announcements.
  • Deliver notifications.
  • Improve app performance.
  • Maintain community safety.
  • Process subscriptions.

6. Community Moderation

To help maintain a respectful university community, users can:

  • Report posts.
  • Report comments.
  • Block other users.

Reported content may be reviewed by authorized administrators.

7. Administrator Dashboard

Premier University Central includes a separate administrator dashboard application used only by authorized administrators.

The administrator dashboard is not available to regular users and is used solely for:

  • Managing users.
  • Reviewing reported content.
  • Removing content that violates community guidelines.
  • Publishing announcements.
  • Monitoring the health of the community.

8. Notifications

The App may send notifications regarding announcements, messages, community activity, and important updates.

Notification history may be stored locally on your device for your convenience.

9. Official Website Links

The App may contain links to official Premier University websites or other trusted resources.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

10. Account Deletion

Users may permanently delete their account directly from within the application. For detailed instructions on how to remove your data, please see our Account Deletion Instructions.

When an account is deleted:

  • Your authentication account will be removed.
  • Your personal information will be deleted where applicable.
  • Some content may remain if required for moderation, legal obligations, or maintaining the integrity of community discussions.

11. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures together with Firebase services to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure.

12. Children's Privacy

This App is intended only for Premier University students and is not directed toward children under the age of 13. Please review our Child Safety Policy for more information.

13. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page along with the updated revision date.
